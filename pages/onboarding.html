Mr. Robot Onboarding Email

Description:

An onboarding email for the fictional hacker group, Fsociety welcoming the recipient and providing the next steps for new members. The tone reflects Mr. Robot's murky, reticent voice while maintaining a professional and concise onboarding format.

Subject: Welcome to fsociety

Hello Elliot,

Welcome to fsociety. We’re looking forward to partnering with you in our takedown of Evil Corp.

To get everything in motion, here’s a quick outline of what to expect next:

  • Your Kickoff Call: You’ll receive a separate calendar invitation from one of our associates. Details will be provided on the location and time.
  • Account Access: Keep an eye out for another email with instructions on how to access your client portal and any other tools we'll be using.
  • Our Process: In the meantime, feel free to review our manifesto, which details the grasp the world’s largest conglomerate has and why we intend to stop their destruction.
